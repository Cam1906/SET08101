Classic Cheese

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 slice of tomato
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 1/8 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1-2 pickles
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon vegetable oil

METHOD

  1. Preheat grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, season the ground beef with salt and pepper. Shape the beef into a patty.
  3. Brush the vegetable oil on both sides of the patty, and grill or cook on a skillet for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until it is cooked to your preferred temperature.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese on top of the patty, and let it melt.
  5. While the burger is cooking, prepare the toppings. Slice the tomato, pickles, and red onion.
  6. To assemble the burger, place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of the hamburger bun. Place the cooked patty with melted cheese on top of the lettuce.
  7. Add a slice of tomato and a few slices of red onion on top of the patty.
  8. Top with 1-2 pickles, and finish with the top half of the bun.
  9. Serve immediately, and enjoy your delicious burger with red onions, cheese, pickles, tomato, and lettuce!
