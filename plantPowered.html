GRAB A BYTE

HOME CLASSIC CHEESE BANGIN BBQ CRISPY CHICKEN INFERNO MEDITERRANEAN

Plant Powered

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • A handful of lettuce leaves
  • A handful of rocket
  • 3-4 thinly sliced radishes
  • 2 tablespoons mayo
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

METHOD

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, drained and rinsed black beans, grated carrots, chopped parsley, chopped cilantro, chopped scallions, minced garlic, olive oil, ground cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix the ingredients together until well combined.
  3. Use your hands to form the mixture into four evenly-sized patties.
  4. Place the patties in the refrigerator for 30 minutes to an hour to firm up.
  5. Heat a grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.
  6. Grill the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are heated through and slightly charred on the outside.
  7. Toast the hamburger bun until it is lightly browned.
  8. Spread mayo on the bottom half of the bun.
  9. Add a layer of lettuce leaves and rocket on top of the mayo.
  10. Place one of the plant-based patties on top of the greens.
  11. Add the thinly sliced radishes on top of the patty.
  12. Finish by placing the top half of the bun on top of the radishes.
  13. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious plant-powered burger!
