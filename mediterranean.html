Mediterranean

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1/4 cup canned sweetcorn, drained
  • 1/4 cup grated zucchini
  • 2 tablespoons hummus
  • A handful of lettuce leaves
  • 2-3 slices of red onion
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

METHOD

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb, minced garlic, chopped parsley, chopped mint, ground cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Use your hands to mix the ingredients together until well combined.
  3. Form the lamb mixture into a patty shape that is slightly larger than the size of the hamburger bun, and make a small indentation in the center of the patty with your thumb. This will help the patty cook evenly and prevent it from puffing up.
  4. Heat a grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the lamb patty for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until it is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  6. While the patty is cooking, drain the sweetcorn and grate the zucchini.
  7. Toast the hamburger bun until it is lightly browned.
  8. Spread hummus on the bottom half of the bun.
  9. Add a layer of lettuce leaves and red onion slices on top of the hummus.
  10. Place the cooked lamb patty on top of the lettuce and onion.
  11. Add the sweetcorn and grated zucchini on top of the patty.
  12. Finish by placing the top half of the bun on top of the vegetables.
  13. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Mediterranean lamb burger!
