Crispy Chicken

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 2-3 slices of red onion
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: sliced tomato or pickles

METHOD

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breast with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the chicken breast for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until it is cooked through.
  4. While the chicken is cooking, toast the hamburger bun on the grill or in a toaster until it is lightly browned.
  5. Spread mayonnaise on both halves of the bun.
  6. Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom half of the bun.
  7. Add the cooked chicken breast on top of the lettuce.
  8. Top the chicken with a few slices of red onion.
  9. Add any additional toppings, such as sliced tomato or pickles, if desired.
  10. Finish the burger by placing the top half of the bun on top.
  11. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious chicken burger with lettuce, mayo, and red onion!
  12. Note: You can also cook the chicken in a skillet or bake it in the oven if you don't have a grill or grill pan.
