Bangin BBQ

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup pulled pork
  • 1 tablespoon BBQ sauce
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 2-3 slices of red onion
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

METHOD

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a mixing bowl, season the ground beef with salt and pepper. Shape the beef into a patty.
  3. Grill the patty for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until it is cooked to your preferred temperature.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of the patty, and let it melt.
  5. While the patty is cooking, cook the bacon until it is crispy. Set it aside on paper towels to drain excess grease.
  6. Toast the hamburger bun on the grill until it is lightly browned.
  7. Spread BBQ sauce on the bottom half of the bun.
  8. Place a lettuce leaf on top of the BBQ sauce.
  9. Add the cooked patty with melted cheese on top of the lettuce.
  10. Top the patty with bacon, pulled pork, and a few slices of red onion.
  11. Finish the burger by placing the top half of the bun on top.
  12. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious BBQ burger with pulled pork!
