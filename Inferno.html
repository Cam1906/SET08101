Inferno

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (such as cheddar or pepper jack)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 2-3 slices of red onion
  • 1-2 pickled jalapenos, sliced
  • 1 small chili pepper
  • 1 skewer
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lettuce for topping

METHOD

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into a patty and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the burger patty for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until it is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  4. While the burger is cooking, toast the hamburger bun on the grill or in a toaster until it is lightly browned.
  5. Place the cooked burger patty on the bottom half of the bun.
  6. Top the burger patty with shredded cheese and chopped chipotle peppers.
  7. Add a few slices of red onion and pickled jalapeno slices on top of the cheese.
  8. Thread the small chili pepper onto the skewer and place the skewer on top of the burger.
  9. Top the burger with lettuce.
  10. Finish the burger by placing the top half of the bun on top.
  11. Serve immediately and enjoy your spicy Inferno burger!
